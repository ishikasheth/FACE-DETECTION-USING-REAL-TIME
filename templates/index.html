Welcome to the Face Recognition App!

Use the navigation bar to capture images, train the model, or recognize faces.

This system uses OpenCV for face detection and LBPH for recognition.

Instructions:

  1. Go to "Capture Faces" to add images of individuals.
  2. Go to "Train Model" after capturing images for all individuals.
  3. Use "Recognize from Image" or "Real-time Recognition" to test.
